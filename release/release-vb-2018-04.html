Release VB-2018-04

Submitted by rwieck on Tue, 2018-04-17 11:54
We are pleased to announce the April 2018 release of VectorBase (VB-2018-04):
  • Many large (and small) datasets have been added to the Population Biology database, including abundance data from four counties in Florida, additional years of data from Southern Nevada, and entomological monitoring data from Uganda. In total 1.6 million new data records have been added, representing 16 million individual vector specimens.
  • The timelime plots in the PopBio map are more responsive and now show vector abundance averaged over year, month, week or day, as appropriate. Currently our most longitudinal dataset is from Southern Nevada, with nine consecutive years of data.
  • New variation data have been added for
    1. Culex quinquefasciatus (two SNPs implicated in insecticide resistance from the paper by Walter Fabricio Silva Martins et al, "Local selection in the presence of high levels of gene flow: Evidence of heterogeneous insecticide selection pressure across Ugandan Culex quinquefasciatus populations", PLOS, Neglected Tropical Diseases doi.org/10.1371/journal.pntd.0005917).
    2. Biomphalaria glabrata variants derived from the Bge3 cell line from the paper currently in preparation "Sequence and structural variation in the genome of the Biomphalaria glabrata embryonic (Bge) cell line". (Nicolas J. Wheeler, Nathalie Dinguirard, Joshua Marquez, Adrian Gonzalez, Mostafa Zamanian, Timothy P. Yoshino, Maria G. Castillo)
    3. New variation data for the G3 A. gambiae colony provided by Tony Nolan, Imperial College, London.
  • Four new differential expression datasets added, including species Biomphalaria glabrata and Cimex lectularius.
  • New whole genome alignment for Aedes aegypti AaegL5 vs Aedes albopictus, here a sample region in Chromosome 2
  • There are two new video tutorials available, PopBio overview and PopBio insecticide resistance view.
  • Unlike our current practice exercises, we now have a new tutorial integrating most major tools with (four) sample cases. The answer key is provided.
  • The ~80 FAQs have been revised, of these seven questions not relevant or valid any more have been deleted. The FAQs categories updated included General, Site usage, Browser, Expression data, Compara and Community manual annotation with Apollo.

Expression data

Four new experiments have been processed and added to the expression browser:

  • Resistance to Schistosoma mansoni (Tennessen et al., 2015) Biomphalaria glabrata RNA-seq experiment
  • Pyrethroid resistance (Adelman et al., 2011) Cimex lectularius RNA-seq experiment
  • Responses of the mosquito to the Zika Virus infection (Etebari et al., 2017) Aedes aegypti RNA-seq experiment
  • Modulation of blood seeking, & susceptibility to infection (Emami et al., 2017) Anopheles gambiae RNA-seq experiment
